About

Hi I'm Timo, Information/UI/UX Designer based in Stuttgart, Germany. Currently I study Information Design at Stuttgart Media University. Within my studying I discovered my interest in crafting interactive experiences. I set my focus on learning how to come up with great product concepts, designing beautiful user interfaces and deliver engaging user experiences.

Foundation

The foundation of that all is an deep understanding of usability. The user is the center of every product and has to be the center of the whole design process. My workflow contains all parts of the user-centered design process, starting with research to find out the needs of the users – designing and prototyping the product – and finally evaluating the prototype and iterating the process until it's finished and has a great user experience.
